📖 Game Guide

How to Play

Everything you need to know before the first frame drops.

Step-by-step
01
🖼️

A Frame / Dialogue Appears

A still image or famous dialogue from a movie or TV show is revealed. No title, no hints — just guess!

02
⏱️

Timer Starts

A countdown begins the moment the frame appears. Shout the answer before time runs out!

03
👨‍⚖️

Judge Phase

When time runs out a Judge is spotlighted. They decide which player shouted correctly first.

04
🎯

Award Points

Tap the winning player's name on the scoring screen. Points land instantly on the board.

05
🔄

Judge Rotates

Every few rounds a new Judge is randomly selected so everyone gets a turn to referee.

06
🏆

Highest Score Wins

After all sections the player with the most points wins. Ties? The Judge breaks them.

💡 Pro Tips
🎙️Shout clearly — the Judge needs to hear you over the crowd.
👁️Look for lighting, costumes and set design — not just faces.
⚡First correct shout wins — speed matters as much as knowledge.
🤫Don't reveal partial answers — someone might steal your point!
